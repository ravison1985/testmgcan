MG 5D Line Charts - User Guide
What are 5D Line Charts?
5D Charts: Show the price movement of each stock over the last 5 trading days as a line chart. This helps you quickly see trends and patterns.
Real-Time Updates: Charts update every few seconds with live price data during market hours.
Color Coding
Green Cards: Stocks that gained over the 5-day period (5D percentage is positive)
Red Cards: Stocks that lost over the 5-day period (5D percentage is negative)
Line Color: Chart line matches the card color (green for gains, red for losses)
Flash Animations (Breaking High/Low)
Green Flashing Border: Stock price just broke above its 5-day high! This means it's at the strongest point in 5 days.
Red Flashing Border: Stock price just broke below its 5-day low! This means it's at the weakest point in 5 days.
Auto-Remove: Flash animation automatically stops after 2 minutes to keep the view clean.
Move Badges
5D Badge: Shows the percentage change over the 5-day period
Mid Move: Stock moved between 2% and 5% in 5 days (moderate movement)
Big Move: Stock moved more than 5% in 5 days (significant movement)
Live Updates: Badges update in real-time as prices change during market hours
Card Information
Symbol: Stock trading symbol (e.g., RELIANCE, TCS, INFY)
LTP: Last Traded Price - the current price of the stock
% Change: Percentage change in price today
5D Line Chart: Visual representation of price movement over 5 days
Search & Stats
Search Bar: Type any stock symbol (e.g., RELIANCE, TCS, INFY) to quickly find and filter stocks
Total Stocks: Total number of F&O stocks displayed
5D Gainers: Number of stocks that gained over the 5-day period (green cards)
5D Losers: Number of stocks that lost over the 5-day period (red cards)

MG 5D Line Charts User Guide
Overview

MG 5D Line Charts display 5-day price movement patterns for all F&O stocks with mini line charts showing recent price trends and key technical levels.

Key Features
  • 5-Day Line Charts: Visual representation of price movement over last 5 trading days
  • Live LTP Updates: Real-time Last Traded Price updates via WebSocket
  • Percentage Change: Intraday % change with color-coded indicators
  • High/Low Markers: Visual indicators for 5-day high and low breaks
  • Index Tracking: Quick view of major indices (NIFTY, BANK NIFTY, etc.)
Understanding the Charts
  • Line Color: Blue line shows price progression over 5 days
  • Green Badge: Stock is showing positive % change today
  • Red Badge: Stock is showing negative % change today
  • Glowing Border (Green): Stock broke above 5-day high - bullish signal
  • Glowing Border (Red): Stock broke below 5-day low - bearish signal
Visual Indicators
  • Pulsing Animation: Indicates a 5-day high or low breakout in progress
  • Move Badge: Shows significant intraday movement (>1.5%)
  • Big Move Badge: Highlights major moves (>3%) requiring attention
How to Use
  • Quick Scan: Rapidly identify trending stocks by chart patterns
  • Breakout Detection: Look for glowing borders to spot breakouts
  • Search Function: Type symbol name to filter specific stocks
  • Indices Section: Monitor overall market direction before trading
  • Pattern Recognition: Identify uptrends, downtrends, and consolidations
