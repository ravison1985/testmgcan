Price Metrics
LTP: Last Traded Price - the current price of the stock
Change: How much the price changed today (in rupees and percentage)
% Open: How far the current price is from the opening price
% High: How far the current price is from today's highest price (negative means below high)
% Low: How far the current price is from today's lowest price (positive means above low)
RVOL: Relative Volume - compares today's trading volume to the average. Higher means more interest in the stock today.
RVOL Colors
Blue Gradient (>3x): Extreme volume - very high trading activity
Orange (2-3x): High volume - strong trading activity
Yellow (1-2x): Medium volume - normal to above average
White (<1x): Low volume - below average trading
Search & Stats

Stats Bar: Shows total stocks, number of gainers (green), and losers (red)

MG Heatmap User Guide
Overview

MG Heatmap visualizes all F&O stocks performance with color-coded heatmap showing intraday percentage changes at a glance.

Key Features
  • Color-Coded Heatmap: Green (positive), Red (negative) with intensity based on % change
  • Real-time Updates: Live price and percentage updates via WebSocket
  • Grid Layout: All stocks displayed in responsive grid format
  • Quick Visual Analysis: Instantly identify market leaders and laggards
Color Coding
  • Dark Green: Strong gainers (>2% up)
  • Light Green: Moderate gainers (0-2% up)
  • Light Red: Moderate losers (0-2% down)
  • Dark Red: Strong losers (>2% down)
